Roti Canai

Description

As the name suggests, roti canai is a flatbread packed with the goodness of eggs and essential fat from the Indian clarified butter, ghee. It has an outer texture of a flaky pastry but is soft and wholesome on the inside. This can be enjoyed on its own or with a spicy curry of one's choice.

Ingredient

  • 1kg wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt

steps

  1. Combine milk, sugar, salt and eggs with water. Stir until dissolved.
  2. Add flour and margarine. Knead until it becomes dough then rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Then knead the dough once more, then take a short break followed by kneading again until the dough is smooth, elastic and chewy.
  4. For this dough, if it's soft, sprinkle enough flour and knead until you get an elastic texture. If the dough looks hard, sprinkle a little water and knead until the dough looks soft.
  5. When the dough is perfect, round the dough then apply margarine on the surface. Put it in the container.
